About me

I’m extremely creative Android Developer from the North-East of Ukraine. I don’t just think outside the box, I live there. Taking a creative approach and using unorthodox methods to reach passionate individuals and small startup companies’ goals. It means that not only I do work hard but I like having fun while I do so and my clients are satisfied. I specialize in responsive web design and mobile application development. I keep abreast of current trends in design and interaction. I am currently employed as a mentor in web design. I teach HTML5, CSS3 (Javascript, Jquery – introduction). But nevertheless I would like to grow as an Android Developer. Day by day I’m getting closer to the true essence of the mobile apps development world and share progress with my clients. You can check up some of my projects here I want my work to serve the overall vision of each given project, set the right mood for it but really, I do enjoy thoroughly all aspects of the process and I believe my work shows both my versatility as well as my desire to grow as a specialist in Android development area. Would like to help you in making an outstanding product. Let’s keep in touch
